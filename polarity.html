Polarity

Choosing a path of either service to others (positive polarity) or service to self (negative polarity) is central to our spiritual growth.

Polarity is the concept of choosing between two paths: service to others (positive polarity) or service to self (negative polarity). This choice is fundamental to our spiritual growth and evolution.

Understanding Polarity

Polarity represents the dual nature of our choices and actions. By choosing the path of service to others, we align ourselves with love, compassion, and unity. Conversely, the path of service to self focuses on power, control, and separation. Understanding and choosing our polarity is a crucial aspect of our spiritual journey.
